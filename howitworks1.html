HOW THE RENTAL ROOM WORKS

CHOOSE A DRESS
Browse through our collection of designer dresses to find your desired style and fit to make you feel like a princess.
SELECT A DATE
Select your preferred rental date with fingers crossed it’s available. If the date is shaded it means it’s unavailable.
SELECT YOUR PAYMENT METHOD
Complete our simple payment process by entering your full details, and then your payment option, we accept VISA and Mastercard payments.
WAIT FOR ARRIVAL
Your garment will arrive at least one working day before your event.
ROCK YOUR OUTFIT
Don’t forget to take a photo and tag us on Instagram - @therentalroom
SEND IT BACK
Simply return it in the prepaid bag it arrived with and it over to a cashier at NZ Post on the day your rental ends. Don’t be late as a fee will apply.
